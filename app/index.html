Emergency SOS

You must send it. DeySafe saves a durable SOS, asks the operator room to alert the trusted circle YOU added, and prepares your SOS + location to share. Only YOU choose who to share with by tapping share. It does not auto-deliver to authorities and never auto-dispatches armed responders.

Add the people who should be alerted when you raise an SOS. Stored privately on this device; the operator room sends to them on your behalf. Add their phone (with country code, e.g. +234…) or WhatsApp.
